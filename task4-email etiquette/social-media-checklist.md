***Social Media Do's and Dont's***

* DO'S

  1.Secure accounts with 'two -step authentication'

  2.Use strong and unique passwords

  3.Only click on links that you are familiar with and trust

  4.Seperate buisness and personal things

  5.Respect other's opinions and privacy .


* DONT'S
 
  1.Don't share personal information : your home address, passwords , etc.

  2.Do not post offensive and abusive content

  3.Don't ignore or be unaware of privacy settings
 
  4.Avoid accepting requests from unknown people
 
  5.Don't use same password for every site.
