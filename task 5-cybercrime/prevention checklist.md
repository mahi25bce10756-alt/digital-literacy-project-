***Prevention Checklist:***

1. Don't click on unknown sites that look suspicious. 
2. Use a strong and unique password.Use diffrent passwords for diffrent accounts.  
3. Keep personal information strictly private. 
4. Enable extra security features - Turn on two-factor authentication for important accounts.
5. Do not scan QR codes from unknown sources
6. Double-check the receiver’s name and details before confirming any transaction.
7. Try to avoid using public Wifi for important logins such as bancking accounts. 
8. Do Not share your OTP , passwords etc to anyone. even to close friends and colleagues. 
* If you suspect or get caught in a fraud,immediatly report it through the National Cyber Crime Portal : cybercrime.gov.in
* For quicker actions you can dial the cybercrime helpline number: 1930
